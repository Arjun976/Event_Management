Common Issues & Solutions

Can't find available dates?

If your preferred dates aren't showing as available:

  • • Try alternative dates within your flexible range
  • • Contact us for last-minute cancellations
  • • Consider weekday events for better availability

Payment not processing?

If you're having payment issues:

  • • Check your card details and billing address
  • • Ensure sufficient funds are available
  • • Try a different payment method

Not receiving confirmation emails?

If you're missing important emails:

  • • Check your spam/junk folder
  • • Verify your email address is correct
  • • Add us to your email whitelist

Need to modify or cancel?

For booking changes or cancellations:

  • • Contact us at least 48 hours in advance
  • • Check our cancellation policy
  • • Have your booking reference ready

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an event? ▼

You can book an event by contacting us through our website form, phone, or visiting us in person. Our team will guide you through the entire process.

Do you offer custom wedding packages? ▼

Yes, we tailor wedding packages to fit your preferences and budget — from venue to décor, catering, and entertainment.

Are your services available outside the city? ▼

Absolutely! We're available for destination events. Travel charges may apply depending on the location.

What types of workshops do you manage? ▼

We manage educational, motivational, and corporate skill-building workshops with expert facilitators.

What is your cancellation policy? ▼

Cancellations made 30+ days in advance receive full refund. 15-29 days: 50% refund. Less than 15 days: 25% refund.

Do you provide equipment and decorations? ▼

Yes, we provide complete event equipment including sound systems, lighting, furniture, and custom decorations.
